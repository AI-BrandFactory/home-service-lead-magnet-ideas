# 12 Lead Magnet Ideas for Home Service

12 working lead magnet ideas for home service businesses, an ideation template that lets you spawn another 12 for any vertical you're in, format specs, the local-SEO multiplication layer, distribution channels with conversion benchmarks, a 6-week launch plan, and 8 mistakes that tank conversion. Built for HVAC, roofing, plumbing, lawn care, pest control, electrical, deck/patio, painting, garage door, windows, solar, and pool operators.

## What's Inside

- Why the home service buyer converts differently in 2026. Local, urgent, comparing 2 to 3 vendors on a five-figure decision. The 5 magnet patterns that work (calculators, decision support, local checklists, diagnostics, maintenance calendars) and the 5 that flop here.
- The 12 lead magnet ideas, each with a format spec, benchmark, three worked examples, and the headline pattern that sells the click. Cost calculator, repair-vs-replace quiz, timeline estimator, materials guide, permit sheet, vendor vetting, maintenance calendar, diagnostic, rebate finder.
- The ideation template. The 5-step framework that generates 12 magnet ideas for any vertical. List the buyer's 5 biggest decisions, map the data each needs, match to a format via lookup table, score by potential x difficulty, ship top 3. Walked through for a garage door installer.
- Format specs cheat sheet. For each of the 5 main formats (calculator, quiz, guide, checklist, diagnostic): page count, field count, conversion benchmark range, build time estimate, software stack with prices. Built so you can scope a magnet realistically before you start.
- The local-SEO multiplication layer. How to take one magnet and spin it into 12 to 50 service-area pages with zip-customized data. The naming convention, 3 technical options (hand-built, programmatic CMS, content layer), the 300-word floor that prevents de-indexing, and schema rules.
- Distribution: 7 channels with benchmarks. Homepage CTA (4 to 8 percent), exit-intent (2 to 5 percent), blog sidebar (6 to 12 percent), Google Ads (12 to 25 percent), Facebook ads (8 to 15 percent), GBP posts, neighborhood groups. Math showing $40K to $180K monthly from one calculator.
- The 6-week launch plan. Week 1 ideation using the Part 3 template. Week 2 build the top idea. Week 3 ship and chase first 100 leads. Week 4 measure and iterate (one fix per week). Weeks 5 and 6 build idea #2 and spin idea #1 across service-area pages. Post-week-6 sustaining cadence.
- The 8 common mistakes that tank home service lead magnets. Too many gate fields, buried value, no follow-up email, generic copy ignoring the local buyer, wrong format, no schema markup, asking the zip and not using it, requiring phone too early. Plus a bonus mobile-QA failure mode.
